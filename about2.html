About

Discover the Soul Behind the Stanzas

As a poet, my mission is to weave together the fragments of feeling and thought into something beautiful and meaningful. My journey into writing began in the depths of my darkest state, where I grappled with fear and trauma stemming from betrayal. It was during these tumultuous moments that I discovered writing as a lifeline—a way to articulate the ineffable and transform pain into art.

Each poem is a heartfelt expression of the shadows and light that dance in my soul, shaped by the quiet whispers of the night and the distant glow of the moon. In this space, I welcome you to explore the inspirations that fuel my creativity, my writing process, and the experiences that have left an indelible mark on my journey as a poet.

Inspiration

My poetry is deeply rooted in personal experience, shaped by moments of vulnerability, healing, and reflection.

I draw from my own experiences—particularly the challenges I faced during my darkest hours, where betrayal and fear threatened to silence my voice. It is through the act of writing that I found a way to not only express my pain but to also find solace and clarity.
