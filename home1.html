"Whispers and Cries: A Poet’s Letters to the Moon"

“In the quiet of the night, my heart speaks—each poem a whisper, each letter a cry to the distant moon.”

Welcome to Whispers, Cries, and Letters to Luna!

Here, you will find a collection of verses born from broken times, dark hours, and the deep longing that threads through our human experience. Each poem is a whisper sent to the moon—a reflection of the emotions that shape our lives. Join me as we explore the landscapes of heartache, hope, and healing through the power of words.

Listen to the whispers.
